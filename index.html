Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Delovu Labs builds apps and tools for e-commerce merchants. This policy applies to all apps published by Delovu Labs, including apps distributed through the Shopify App Store.

Data we collect

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you, your customers, or your store visitors. App configuration (such as announcement bar settings) is stored entirely within your Shopify theme and is managed by Shopify.

Third-party services

Our apps operate within the Shopify platform. Shopify's own privacy practices govern any data processed during app installation and authentication. You can review Shopify's privacy policy at shopify.com/legal/privacy.

Cookies and tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies on your storefront or in our apps.

Data retention

Because we do not collect data, there is nothing to retain. Uninstalling an app removes all associated theme code from your store.
